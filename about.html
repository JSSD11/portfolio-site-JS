About Me

"To design an experience is to experience the design."

scroll down

Always iterating. Always improving. Forever learning.

Great design involves iterating and improving, and my journey into UX design has followed a similar approach. Over the years, a curious nature coupled with an ability to learn and adapt quickly, have enabled me to cross both borders and industries. From engineering to business development, to graphic design, each of my roles have an underlying passion connecting them: a need to understand how humans work and a desire to solve their problems.

I started out as a structural engineer and soon ventured into the world of business. Along the way, I discovered the joys of graphic design in helping to turn people’s stories into beautiful messages. However, I desired something more – which is where UX Design comes in.

UX Design is the perfect bridge between my technical, analytical, business, and creative minds. Indeed, UX Design is where I find my flow. Through UX Design I am committed to improve and enrich how people interact with technology so they can work smarter and live happier. I love getting under the hood of problems and figuring out how best to connect the dots so that the system (human + technology) works in harmony - only then can I hope to make people’s lives simpler, more effective, and more fun.

My journey may have its twists, but those twists have made me dynamic and adaptable. Using my diverse experience, I am able to bridge the gap between business goals and customer needs, always working to ensure that no story goes unheard and no problem goes unsolved. I now use design to turn people’s stories into meaningful experiences.

Why work with me?

I'm personable, down-to-earth and patient. I do coffee dates and real conversation. I love working closely with clients and building long-lasting relationships. I make sure to give each project the care and attention it needs to ensure I deliver high-quality work that delights in both its function and aesthetic.

I love what I do.

I mostly enjoy the process of crafting solutions to problems that ultimately deliver business value. I believe that, through in-depth user research and exploration, the right solution will present itself. I love using multiple tools and testing various iterations until I have developed . It’s about reaching that “AhA!” moment, where the user not only understands the product, but also can see how it will improve their lives.
