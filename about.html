My Story

Hi I’m Joanne, a UX/UI designer currently based in London; but this has not always been the case. Over the years, my curious nature, along with my ability to learn and adapt quickly, have enabled me to cross both borders and industries.

I started out as a structural engineer and soon ventured into the world of business. Along the way, I discovered the joys of graphic design in helping turn people’s stories into beautiful messages. However, I desired something more – this is where UX Design comes in. It is the perfect bridge between my technical, analytical, business, and creative minds. Indeed, UX Design is where I find my flow.

Through UX Design I am committed to improve and enrich how people interact with technology so they can work smarter and live happier. I love getting under the hood of problems and figuring out how best to connect the dots so that the system (human + technology) works in harmony - only then can I achieve my goal of turning people’s stories into meaningful experiences.

Why work with me?

I'm personable, down-to-earth and patient. I do coffee dates and real conversation. I love working closely with clients and building long-lasting relationships. I make sure to give each project the care and attention it needs to ensure I deliver high-quality work that delights in both its function and aesthetic.

I love what I do.

I mostly enjoy the process of crafting solutions to problems that ultimately deliver business value. I believe that, through in-depth user research and exploration, the right solution will present itself. I love using multiple tools and testing various iterations until I have developed . It’s about reaching that “AhA!” moment, where the user not only understands the product, but also can see how it will improve their lives.
