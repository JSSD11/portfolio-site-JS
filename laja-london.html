OVERVIEW

LAJA LONDON is a premium, lifestyle fashion brand for men that commits to deliver modern, sophisticated, and inspirational clothing, with a focus on design and quality. To Laja London, design is as much about the head-to-toe look as it is about creating functional, practical clothes that fit in with our busy lifestyles. They achieve this through emphasis on good quality fabrics, construction, fit, and an attention to detail.

Additionally Laja London encourages a healthy lifestyle by embodying the values and aspirations of healthy living through the notion of feeling and looking as good inside as out.

I was commissioned by Laja London to design and develop their e-commerce website. The objective was for the website to portray the brand's key values of quality, practicality, and attention to detail.

MY ROLE

I was responsible for the UX and UI design of the ecommerce site, as well as the front-end development servivces.

The following outlines the key objectives to the brief:

  • A visually appealing online store;
  • Highly secure and reliable;
  • Mobile responsiveness;
  • Easy payment service to buyers, allowing theem to pay using different payment options;
  • An integrated, easy-to-use inventory management system;
  • SEO friendly;
  • Excellent loading speed;

Considering time and resource constraints, we decided to use the Shopify platform to facilitate the building of the website. However, rather than using a cookie-cutter website template, I decided to fully customize the website as none of the free themes provided the desired user experience.
